About
Cables Pte Ltd

Cables Pte Ltd, established in 2016, is an ISO 9001:2015 certified cable manufacturer in Fiji. Quality Assurance

At Cables Pte Ltd, quality is at the heart of everything we do. Our products are manufactured to Australia/New Zealand standards and certified by NATA and IANZ-accredited laboratories.

With an in-house, fully equipped testing laboratory, we rigorously test raw materials and finished products to meet stringent quality standards. We are proud holders of the ISO 9001:2015 certification, ensuring consistency and excellence in every cable we manufacture.
